Homely Buys Logo Design

Homely Buys was an android project taken up by a friend. The idea was to create on-demand service that connects consumers with local stores.

I came up with the idea of showing a house top and a bread which is one of the most essential grocery item. The bread is drawn out in a way it looks like alphabet “B”. B for bread and also B for Buys. The logo should be able to convey the meaning of being relaxed at home while getting all the daily household essentials delivered at home. Hence, being true to its name “HomelyBuys”
